Return Policy

We understand that sometimes you may need to return a product, and we want to make the process as easy as possible. Below is our return policy:

Eligibility for Returns

  • Products must be returned within 30 days of the delivery date.
  • The product must be unused, in its original condition, and in the original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required for all returns.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Gift cards.
  • Items marked as "final sale" or "non-returnable" at the time of purchase.

Return Process

To return a product, follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team via email or phone to initiate the return process.
  2. Provide your order number, the product you're returning, and the reason for the return.
  3. Once approved, you will receive a return shipping label. Please package the item securely and send it back to us.
  4. Once we receive the returned item, we will process your refund or exchange.

Refunds

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method once the return is processed. Please allow up to 7 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange a product, please follow the return process and then place a new order for the desired item. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

If you have any questions regarding our return policy, feel free to contact our customer service team.
